─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. REFUND POLICY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Sage Analytics offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans.
Customers who cancel within 30 days of their initial purchase will
receive a full refund, no questions asked.

After 30 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
Partial refunds may be issued for unused subscription periods at
management's discretion. Annual subscribers who cancel after 30 days
are eligible for a pro-rated refund for remaining months.

To request a refund, email billing@sage-analytics.com with:
  - Your account email address
  - Your customer ID (found in Account Settings)
  - Reason for cancellation (optional)

Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days and returned
to the original payment method.

3. DATA PRIVACY & SECURITY POLICY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Sage Analytics takes data privacy seriously and complies with:
  - GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) — EU customers
  - CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) — US customers
  - SOC 2 Type II Certified

All uploaded documents are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in
transit using TLS 1.3. Customer data is never shared with third
parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention:
  - Active accounts: data retained indefinitely
  - Cancelled accounts: data deleted after 90 days
  - Backups purged within 180 days of cancellation

Customers may request a full data export at any time via the
Account Settings > Data Export page, or by emailing privacy@sage-analytics.com.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Users may NOT use Sage Analytics to:
  - Upload content that violates copyright laws
  - Process personal health information (PHI) unless on a HIPAA-compliant plan
  - Attempt to reverse-engineer the AI models
  - Conduct automated scraping beyond documented API rate limits
  - Upload malware or malicious files

Violations may result in immediate account suspension without refund.

6. CANCELLATION POLICY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Monthly subscribers may cancel at any time. Access continues until
the end of the current billing period.

Annual subscribers cancelling mid-term are subject to the refund
policy outlined in Section 1.

To cancel, navigate to Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan,
or contact support@sage-analytics.com.
