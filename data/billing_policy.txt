SAGA PLATFORM — REFUND & BILLING POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective Date: 1 January 2025
==============================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This document describes the billing practices, refund eligibility, and dispute
resolution procedures for all subscription plans offered on the SAGA platform
(Starter, Pro, and Enterprise).  By subscribing to any SAGA plan you agree to
the terms outlined below.


2. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS
---------------------
SAGA offers three tiers of service:

  a) Starter Plan — $99 / month
     Includes up to 5 users, 1 GB document storage, and 500 AI queries / month.
     Billed monthly. No annual discount available on this tier.

  b) Pro Plan — $499 / month
     Includes up to 25 users, 10 GB document storage, and unlimited AI queries.
     Available monthly or annually (20 % discount on annual pre-pay).

  c) Enterprise Plan — $1,999 / month
     Includes unlimited users, 100 GB document storage, dedicated support, and
     custom SLA.  Annual billing only.  Custom pricing available for > 500 seats.

All prices are in USD and exclude applicable taxes.


3. BILLING CYCLE & PAYMENT
--------------------------
  • Monthly plans are billed on the same calendar day each month (the
    "billing anniversary").
  • Annual plans are billed upfront on the subscription start date.
  • Accepted payment methods: major credit / debit cards, ACH bank transfer
    (US only), and bank wire (Enterprise only).
  • Invoices are emailed to the billing contact within 24 hours of each charge.
  • Overdue invoices accrue a 1.5 % monthly late fee after a 10-day grace period.


4. REFUND POLICY
----------------
  4.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
      New subscribers on the Starter or Pro plan may request a full refund
      within 30 calendar days of their FIRST subscription charge.  To qualify:
        – The account must not have exceeded 100 AI queries during the trial
          period.
        – The refund request must be submitted via the Billing portal or by
          emailing billing@saga-platform.io.
        – Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original
          payment method.

  4.2 Pro-Rata Refunds (Annual Plans)
      If an annual Pro or Enterprise subscriber cancels mid-term, SAGA will
      issue a pro-rata credit for the unused full months remaining.  Partial
      months are not refunded.  Credits are applied to the account balance and
      may be used toward a future subscription; they are not issued as cash.

  4.3 Non-Refundable Items
      The following are non-refundable under all circumstances:
        – Add-on purchases (extra storage blocks, additional seats bought
          mid-cycle).
        – Overage charges incurred beyond plan limits.
        – Setup and onboarding fees for Enterprise accounts.
        – Any subscription period beyond 30 days from first charge (monthly
          plans).

  4.4 Enterprise Refunds
      Enterprise refund requests are handled individually by the Enterprise
      Customer Success team and are subject to the terms agreed in the Master
      Subscription Agreement (MSA).


5. CANCELLATION
---------------
  • Subscribers may cancel at any time through Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the END of the current billing period; access
    is retained until then.
  • Data is retained for 30 days post-cancellation, after which it is
    permanently deleted.  Export your data before cancelling.


6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
---------------------
  If you believe a charge is incorrect:
    1. Contact billing@saga-platform.io within 60 days of the charge date.
    2. Include your account ID, the invoice number, and a description of the
       discrepancy.
    3. Our billing team will investigate and respond within 3 business days.
  Chargebacks initiated without first contacting SAGA may result in account
  suspension pending resolution.


7. PLAN CHANGES (UPGRADES / DOWNGRADES)
----------------------------------------
  • Upgrading mid-cycle: the price difference is charged immediately on a
    pro-rata basis; the new plan takes effect immediately.
  • Downgrading mid-cycle: the change takes effect at the next billing
    anniversary; no partial credit is issued for the remaining days.
